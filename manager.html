Wilmot's 4-Point Rating Scale

Excelling: Consistently goes above and beyond. Sets a strong example and lives out Wilmot's core values in meaningful ways.
Performing: Reliably meets expectations and carries out responsibilities effectively. A strong, steady contributor to the team and culture.
Developing: Progressing in the role with potential. Some areas need more consistency, focus, or support to fully meet expectations.
Underperforming: Struggling to meet expectations in key areas. Needs improvement and may require coaching or more direct support.
